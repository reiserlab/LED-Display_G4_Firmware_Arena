This shows, that the SD card initialization is very fast (79 ms), and the directory scan is the main time consumer (2222 ms for 743 files). The heap usage is temporarily reduced but freed after the scan completes.

```
  SD init: 79 ms
  Free heap after SD init: 537189288 bytes
  --- scanPatternDirectory() ---

    Free heap after alloc: 536861784 bytes
    /patterns/ dir open: 1 ms
    Directory scan (743 files): 2222 ms
    Insertion sort: 0 ms
    Free heap after free: 537188944 bytes
  --- scanPatternDirectory() total: 2223 ms ---
  Free heap after scan: 537189288 bytes
=== SdManager::begin() total: 2302 ms ===
```
